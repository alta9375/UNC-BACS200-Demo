Basic Recording

Steps required

I made this video with the following steps. Use these steps to record and publish a simple YouTube video from your phone.

  • Step 1 - Record. I used an Android phone to record a video.
  • Step 2 - Review. I used the Gallery to view all my videos and replay then for review.
  • Step 3 - Upload. I used the share to YouTube option to upload the video.
  • Step 4 - Publish. Let everything default in YouTube.
  • Step 5 - Play. Visit my YouTube channel to play my video.

YouTube Options

Steps required

There are a few options that you may want to set when you publish your video.

  • Step 1 - Publish Video. Basic Recording steps will get a public YouTube video.
  • Step 2 - Details. Find Channel and Video. Select Details.
  • Step 3 - Title & Description. I used the share to YouTube option to upload the video.
  • Step 4 - Thumbnail. Select the image that shows in the initial view.
  • Step 5 - Audience. "Not made for kids", and "Restrict to over 18" will stop ads from playing.
  • Step 6 - Visibility. "Unlisted" lets you use the video in a page without having it be part of your YouTube channel. "Public" makes it visible in your channel.

More YouTube Options

Steps required

Visit the More Options tab.

  • Step 1 - Comments. "Disable comments" or "Hold all comments for review" to prevent spam.
  • Step 2 - Misc info. Set "Recording Date", "License", "Category".
  • Step 3 - Allow Embedding. Supports the "Copy Embed Code". Adjust the size for the iframe.

Video Edit

Steps required

Use Movie Maker on Windows or iMovie on Mac to edit the video.

  • Step 1 - Download. Save the file from YouTube onto the local computer.
  • Step 2 - Import Video. Import the file into the video editing app.
  • Step 3 - Delete Pauses. Find all the sections where the audio is undesirable. Delete those sections.
  • Step 4 - Share to YouTube. Upload the new file to YouTube. This creates a new video that can be handled as before.
